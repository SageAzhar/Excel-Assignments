1. What do you mean by cells in an excel sheet?

ANS:     A cell in excel sheet is formed by intersection of column and row. It has rectangular shape. 
    They are identified by reference which is formed by combination of column name and row number. 
    For ex., A cell in Column B and Row num 6 would be called "B6".

2. How can you restrict someone from copying a cell from your worksheet?
ANS:    1. Right click on the cell and click on format cells
        2. Click on protection
        3.Ensure Locked is checked and click ok
        4.Use this key combination. Alt+P and then P again. This will give you the Protect Sheet pop up
        5.Remove the check from Select locked cells, type in a password and click ok. retype the password
